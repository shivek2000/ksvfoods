About Us

At KSV Foods

Established in the early 1980s, KSV Caterers is a cherished family-owned business proudly serving the community of Durban and its environs. At KSV, we are dedicated to crafting the freshest and most tantalizing Eastern and Western cuisines, ensuring an unforgettable culinary experience for every occasion. From intimate birthday celebrations to elegant weddings and solemn funerals, our team caters to events of all sizes with impeccable attention to detail. Our diverse menu boasts an array of specialties, including sumptuous sweet and savory platters, aromatic biryani, flavorful curries, succulent roasts, comforting pastas, refreshing salads, and indulgent desserts. Committed to excellence, we take a holistic approach to meal preparation, meticulously sourcing the finest ingredients and delivering exquisite dishes with unparalleled taste and presentation. Experience convenience with our reliable delivery service, ensuring that your culinary desires are met with utmost ease and efficiency.
